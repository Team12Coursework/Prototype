Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

If you haven’t already, you need to register for an account and accept our terms of services. Once you sign in, there will be an option to generate a game for various game modes from on profile page. For in-depth instructions on the game modes, please refer to the Game Rules section above.

Im having trouble with my account

For any questions or issues involving your account please contact our team through our official channels listed in Contact Us, we will respond as promptly as possible.

I’d like to report malicious behaviour of another user.

If another user has been causing trouble through our services, please use our report feature detailing the events and circumstances of the incident, as well as the time that it occurred. Our team of moderators will review and respond to this issue as soon as they are available.

Customer Service

For any queries you may have first refer to our FAQ above first, before contacting us. Any suggestions or feedback are always welcome, please use the forums for this, which our team moderates on a frequent basis. Should you encounter any problems not covered in FAQ, feel free to contact us, we will swiftly respond to you during our working hours.
