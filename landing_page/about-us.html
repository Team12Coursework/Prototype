About Us

Team 12 first met in the university kick-off event and started off by getting to know each other and learning the importance of good teamwork. We also made commitments to the team to ensure we are all dedicated to creating the best game we could. We spoke about the importance of keeping a good atmosphere in the team as it directly correlates to the mood of the team. We spoke about how the atmosphere must be relaxed and comfortable therefore allowing everyone to be open and honest. We also made a clear step by step list to solving conflicts to ensure any problems wouldnâ€™t hindering our progress.

Through weekly team meetings we have all inputted our ideas into creating the perfect game. As a group we identified that there is a lack of games that are not only educational but also enjoyable for users. This helped us unanimously agree on creating CharacterConnect, a game inspired loosely by the concept of scrabble. Not only will it allow students to enjoy friendly competition with friends and classmates, but it will also help them work on their vocabulary skills and improve on their English skills. An important part of primary education for young children involves spelling tests, CharacterConnect will definitely result in students improving on their spelling and literacy, therefore it will have academic benefits.
