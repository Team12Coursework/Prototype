About the game

Welcome to CharacterConnect, a multiplayer word game inspired by Scrabble that combines education and games to foster entertaining learning experience. Our game accommodates for both competitive and casual players, with the later having various game mode that they can engage with their friends. For the more competitive - ranked games are available, that test the wits of players against one another through an Elo and leader board system. Those seeking a fresh experience outside our standard game modes can opt to generate their own unique games with customisable themes, which can be shared throughout our community. We aim to evolve our game further to deliver the best possible experience for our players, so stay tuned for our updates.
