Our Service and Commitments


•Providing an enjoyable, educational game- We at team 12 have created a game not only for you to enjoy but also learn from. We want our user to have an enjoyable experience playing CharacterConnect with their friends whilst also allowing users to improve on their vocabulary and literacy skills in order to boost their academic skills
• Fostering a friendly competitive community spirit- Throughout the features included in our game, we have ensured it allows users to not only communicate but also too bring out their competitive side. The in-game chat box allows this, as it allows students to communicate with each other whilst playing, therefore allowing them to make new friendships and also communicate online to friends and classmates. The leader board will allow students to compete for the top spot, therefore allowing students to engage in competition to finish at the top.
• Providing a safe environment- As our game will have a young audience, we want to make sure that they will be safe from offensive language and bullying, therefore our in-game chat will protect users from seeing bad/ offensive language showing up.
• Keeping your information confidential under the Data Protection Act 2018

CharacterConnect provides a safe environment for all users, however we advise all young players of the game to notify parents/ guardians that you use this game and make sure you are given permission by your parents/ guardians to play.
